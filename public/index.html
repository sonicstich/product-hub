AI documentation search

Stop digging through Notion. Ask your own Claude in plain language — it searches our documentation and answers with links to the source pages.

1
Open your corporate Claude
Go to claude.ai and sign in with your work account.
2
Connect Notion (one time)
In Claude open Settings → Connectors, find Notion, click Connect, and authorize access to the workspace. You only do this once.
3
Ask your question
Start a new chat, make sure the Notion tool is enabled (tools menu), then ask. Claude searches the docs and answers with the source pages linked.
Suggested prompt — copy, paste into Claude, then add your question:
Search our Notion documentation to answer my question. Quote the exact part you used and link the source page(s). If you can't find it in the docs, say so instead of guessing.

My question:
Tips for better answers
  • Be specific — name the area (KYC, Deposits, Withdrawals, Trading…) and the exact feature.
  • Always ask for the source link so you can open the full page.
  • If the answer looks off, reply “search again and check other pages.”
